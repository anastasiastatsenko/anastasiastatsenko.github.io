Technology is political - not neutral.

Personal Secret Agent is a web intervention that explores the intersecting topics of BigTech monopolies, privacy, and the cost of free.

While there are many resources available on making more sustainable choices and lifestyles, few of them address the digital world. Today's crises (housing, food, climate, land, social) are interconnected, and our physical and digital worlds also deeply so—in many ways they coproduce each other.

As a small step towards investgating this complex relationship, Personal Secret Agent challenges you to think about the ways your online habits contribute to and maintain exploitative power dynamics responsible for the degradation of our planetary ecologies. There is immense potential for positive change when scaling individual habits, and the choices we make about the digital products and services we use are no exception.
