Lab 1

Assignment

Create a 1+ page website using semantic elements, external css, responsive images, and flexbox positioning. Use ChatGPT to suggest 5 webproject ideas, include imagery.

ChatGPT prompt

Can you give me 5 creative and artistic ideas for simple static web projects that use html, css and vanilla javascript. The themes of the web projects should be related to internet culture; digital privacy and surveillance; net neutrality; sustainability; data ownership and ethics; digital inclusion and accessibility; and open-source. For each idea, describe what the website is in 1 sentence, and describe what the content will be in 1 sentence. Then, for each idea please suggest a creative direction for imagery, including subject, color, medium, and composition. Provide 1 prompt that I can use with DALL-E to generate an image following your creative direction.

Internet Culture Chronicles

A digital museum showcasing the evolution of internet culture through memes, trends, and iconic moments.

Content

Curated collections of memes, viral videos, and internet phenomena with historical context and significance.

Imagery

Subject: A dynamic collage of iconic memes and symbols.
Color: Vibrant and contrasting colors inspired by popular internet aesthetics.
Medium: Digital illustrations and pixel art.
Composition: Overlapping elements in a chaotic yet organized grid.

DALL-E Prompt

Generate a digital artwork featuring a surreal collage of famous internet memes and symbols with a playful and vibrant color palette.

Guardian of Digital Secrets

An interactive platform educating users about digital privacy threats and empowering them with privacy-enhancing tools.

Content

Informative articles, interactive quizzes, and resources on digital privacy best practices.

Imagery

Color: Cool blues and greens, emphasizing a calm and secure environment.
Medium: Vector graphics for a clean and modern look.
Composition: Clear and concise visuals to convey privacy and security.

DALL-E Prompt

Generate an image illustrating a digital fortress with locks and shields in serene blues and greens, symbolizing online privacy and security.
